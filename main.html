Welcome to my website: I am a PhD candidate at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Auburn University. I completed my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Auburn University in 2022. I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2017. My current research focuses on learning and communication co-design problems for real-time prediction systems. I have explored the impact of fresh features on real-time prediction systems which play crucial role in a wide range of applications such as autonomous automobile systems, medical monitoring, industrial control systems, and robotics systems. My research has brought novel telecommunication framework and advanced scheduling strategies.
